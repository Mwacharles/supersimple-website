INTERNATIONAL SCHOOL OF MUSIC

By Charles, Founder & Buster Moon

My name is Charles Mwangi.
CEO of Electron E Company.
I love music, but I can’t sing.
So, I built a school.
A place for talent to shine.
A stage for creativity.

Music has existed for centuries.
It began with nature’s rhythm.
The hum of the wind.
The beat of the drums.
The songs of early civilizations.
A language beyond words.

Ancient cultures embraced sound.
From Egyptian melodies.
To Greek symphonies.
Music was more than entertainment.
It was spiritual, emotional, cultural.
A force that shaped history.

Over time, music evolved.
The Renaissance brought harmonies.
Classical composers shaped the future.
Then jazz, rock, pop, and hip-hop emerged.
Every era brought new legends.
Every sound told a new story.

Music is beauty in motion.
A simple note can spark emotions.
A melody can heal the soul.
From love songs to battle anthems.
It moves people in ways words cannot.

Stars are born from passion.
Unknown voices become legends.
Hard work turns dreams into reality.
Every star starts small.
But their fire, talent, and persistence make them great.

This school is a gateway.
A chance for hidden stars to rise.
To be heard, seen, and admired.
To transform talent into greatness.
To prove that dreams are worth chasing.

Every weekend, a grand show.
Artists will earn from tickets.
A VIP section will hold celebrity guests.
The world will witness greatness.
Right here, on this stage.

29th May 2025, the grand opening.
Everyone is invited.
A night of music and dreams.
A new era begins.
Join us. Celebrate with us.

The story of music continues.
New stars will rise.
And their voices will change the world.
